Follow the below steps to move files to the pi and then build app on the pi. Run it by double clicking on the app


scp -r "D:\GitHub Projects\FlutterCalendar\skylight_calendar" pi@highlight:~/skylight_calendar

password raspberry

cd ~/skylight_calendar
flutter pub get
flutter build linux --release


on the pi go to skylight_calendar/build/linux/arm64/release/bundle/
double click skylight_calendar
